Last updated: 9th Oct,2023

## 1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data or information from our users. Our application is designed to respect your privacy.

## 2. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to help us understand how our users interact with the app, which helps us improve the user experience. Firebase Analytics collects information such as app usage, performance metrics, and crash events. This data is aggregated and anonymized, so it cannot be used to identify individual users. For more information on Firebase Analytics and how it collects and processes data, please visit [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
